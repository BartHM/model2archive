In article <538@camco.Celestial.COM> bill@camco.Celestial.COM (Bill Campbell) writes:
>Can I use an old style (Model II, Model 16) keyboard on a Tandy
>6000?  I know I have to change the cable.  If it can be used, do
>I just have to make a straight connector cable or change the
>pin-outs?
>
   yes, you can...   use a model 2 cable, and route the conductors
this way...
 
           connector on video/keyboard board
       
                 O  O  O  O  X  O
                 1  2  3  4     6 

           colors of the wires in the model 2 cable...
 
                 white   ->   pin 1
                 red     ->   pin 2
                 black   ->   pin 3
                 green   ->   pin 4
                 yellow  ->   pin 6
